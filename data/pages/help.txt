====== Getting Help ======
OpenNIC provides various contact methods when you need help getting started or just want to chat. Currently those contact methods are:
  * Mailing List: [[https://lists.opennicproject.org/sympa/info/discuss|discuss@lists.opennicproject.org]]
  * IRC: [[https://webchat.freenode.net/?randomnick=1&channels=%23opennic|#opennic]] on the [[https://freenode.net/|freenode IRC network]]
  * Traditional Email: [[support@opennicproject.org]]

Other contact methods which require a working OpenNIC setup on your PC:
  * Forum: [[http://talk.geek/|talk.geek]]
