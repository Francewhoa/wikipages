====== Setting up OpenNIC on macOS ======
  - Click the **Apple Menu** and select **System Preferences** \\ {{:setup:setting_up_opennic_on_macos_1.png?nolink&200|}}
  - Choose the **Network** panel item \\ {{:setup:setting_up_opennic_on_macos_2.png?nolink&400|}}
  - Select your network interface from the left side (//e.g. "Wi-Fi" or "Ethernet"//), then click the **Advanced** button in the lower right corner of the window \\ {{:setup:setting_up_opennic_on_macos_3.png?nolink&400|}}
  - Choose the **DNS** tab at the top of the screen
    - To edit an existing DNS server, double click on the DNS address you wish to change
    - To remove a DNS server, select a DNS address and then click either the minus [-] button or hit the **delete** key \\ {{:setup:setting_up_opennic_on_macos_4.png?nolink&400|}}
    - To add a new DNS server, click plus [+] button, and enter the DNS address. Once you finished making changes to the DNS settings, click the **OK** button \\ {{:setup:setting_up_opennic_on_macos_5.png?nolink&400|}}
  - Now click on **Apply** for the DNS changes to take effect. \\ {{:setup:setting_up_opennic_on_macos_6.png?nolink&400|}}
